Discuss the scope of var, let, and const

In JS var,let and const are used to declare a variables. variables declared by "var" is functional scope means they are accesible from anywhere within the function."var" has Hoisting behaivor.
Variables declared by "let and const" are block scope which means these variables are accesible only from the block they are declared.
